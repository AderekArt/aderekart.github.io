About Us

Digital Monster: Alternative (DM:Alt) is a project dedicated to creating new conceptual versions of Digimon, whether rescuing beta designs or bringing new references to their concepts. DM:Alt aims to revitalize the essence of Digimon, combining creativity and innovation to offer a unique and immersive experience for fans.
